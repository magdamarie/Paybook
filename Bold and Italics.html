Bold and Italics


Bold

How to Bold your text: Use the 'b' tag.

How to Bold your text: You can also use the 'strong' tag.

Strong is better than bold. Strong tells the browser that the information is important. The 'Bold' only styles the text... not what we are going for with html


The 'em' tag is used to Emphasize, which if you ask me is kinda the same thing as italic.

Emphasize is better than italic. Emphasize tells the browser that the information is important. The 'italic' only styles the text... not what we are going for with html

How to Italicise your text: Use the 'i' tag.
